  Completing my unfinished assessments

Assessments must be complete before they can be reviewed and approved by the National Facilitator and only then will be available for viewing on the website. To complete an unfinished assessment, you can find the assessment on the list of Incomplete Assessments that appears on your My Dashboard page or by doing a search using the “Add Assessment” link {link to Assessors’ Guide – Finding my assessments help topic}.

(click to enlarge)

In the Incomplete Assessment grid, click the “Complete my assessment” link and finish answering all the questions in the assessment. When all the questions have been answered, click the “Mark my assessment as complete” box and then click the “Save Assessment” button.

The completed assessment will then automatically show up on the National Facilitator’s dashboard for review and approval.
