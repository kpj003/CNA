  Recent assessments

The Home Page displays the last 12 assessments to have been added or edited, sorted by date with the most recent assessments at the top of the list. If the assessment has subsequently been edited or updated, the date of the last edit will be shown. The list shows scientific name, region or country, assessor’s first initial and last name, and the date that the assessment was created. Clicking on the hyperlinked scientific name will take you to the assessment.
